# Code of Conduct — LYF SOS

LYF SOS is a public safety project.
Respect, responsibility, and empathy are mandatory.

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues.
- Pull requests.
- Discussions.
- Documentation.
- Community interactions.

---

## 🤝 Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a harassment-free, inclusive environment for
everyone, regardless of:

- Experience level.
- Gender identity.
- Sexual orientation.
- Disability.
- Nationality.
- Language.
- Ideology.

---

## ✅ Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intentions.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Prioritize user safety.
- Keep discussions professional.
- Respect privacy and ethics.

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or hate speech.
- Personal attacks.
- Discrimination.
- Threats or intimidation.
- Dismissive or mocking language.
- Pushing unethical features.
- Glorifying surveillance or misuse.

Zero tolerance for abuse.

---

## 🚨 Safety Comes First

Discussions that:
- Encourage misuse.
- Promote panic.
- Suggest unethical surveillance.
- Downplay user consent.

…will be stopped immediately.

---

## 🛡️ Enforcement

Project maintainers may:
- Remove comments.
- Close issues.
- Reject pull requests.
- Temporarily or permanently ban contributors.

Decisions are made to protect:
- Users.
- Project integrity.
- Community safety.

---

## 📬 Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

📧 **help@lyfmail.com**

Reports are reviewed confidentially and seriously.

---

## 🌱 Community Values

LYF SOS values:
- Humility.
- Responsibility.
- Transparency.
- Restraint.
- Human dignity.

---

## ⚠️ Final Reminder

This project deals with **real emergencies and real lives**.

Act accordingly.
